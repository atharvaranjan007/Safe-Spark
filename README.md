# 👟 Safe Spark
### Smart Shoe for Personal Safety

> **Safe Spark** is a wearable safety solution that transforms ordinary footwear into an intelligent personal protection system. It integrates a non-lethal self-defense mechanism, GPS tracking, GSM emergency alerts, and a mini camera to provide immediate assistance during emergencies while collecting digital evidence.

---

# 📌 Problem Statement

Personal safety remains a major challenge worldwide. Every day, people face incidents such as street harassment, stalking, assault, kidnapping, and violence while travelling to schools, colleges, workplaces, or public places.

Existing safety solutions such as pepper sprays, panic buttons, and mobile applications often fail because they:
- Need to be carried separately.
- May not be accessible during panic situations.
- Depend heavily on mobile phones and internet connectivity.
- Cannot simultaneously protect the victim and collect evidence.

Safe Spark addresses these limitations by integrating essential safety technologies into an everyday wearable.

---

# 💡 Solution

Safe Spark is a smart shoe designed to provide immediate protection during emergencies.

The system integrates:

- ⚡ Non-lethal Taser
- 📍 GPS Tracking
- 📶 GSM Emergency Alert System
- 📷 Mini HD Camera
- 🔋 Rechargeable Battery
- 💧 Waterproof Design

Upon activation, the system:
1. Delivers a temporary non-lethal electric shock to the attacker.
2. Starts recording audio and video evidence.
3. Sends live GPS location to registered emergency contacts.
4. Enables quick escape while simultaneously informing others.

The insights obtained helped define the features and overall design of Safe Spark.
